About Our Research

This system proposes the development of a smart, browser-based application that leverages advanced Machine Learning (ML) models—specifically K-Nearest Neighbour (KNN), Random Forest (RF), Naïve Bayes, and Decision Trees—to predict donor–recipient compatibility with unprecedented accuracy.

By analyzing critical medical features such as RhD factor, crossmatch results, and transfusion history, the system actively reduces transfusion errors. It provides a secure, integrated platform for hospital administrators, receptionists, lab technicians, and patients to ensure a safer and more efficient blood management pipeline.
