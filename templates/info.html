Medical Info

Blood transfusion is a life-saving medical procedure, but its success relies heavily on the accurate compatibility of donors and recipients. Traditionally, compatibility is determined using basic ABO and RhD typing, with extended antigen testing in some cases. However, mismatched transfusions still occur, leading to severe issues, transfusion reactions, and complications in patients with chronic transfusion needs such as thalassemia or sickle cell disease.We build an automation for accurate blood donor matching prediction that can be used in a hospitals.

Info

Blood banks and hospitals face a growing demand for safe transfusion practices. While traditional serological methods provide basic compatibility, they do not always account for extended antigens, patient history, and other risk factors. Machine Learning offers the potential to enhance prediction by analysing multiple medical and clinical factors simultaneously. This project focuses on building a smart donor–recipient matching system that improves transfusion outcomes, reduces the risk of complications, and enhances hospital workflow through a user-friendly browser application. .
